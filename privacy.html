Privacy Policy for Kintix

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

At Kintix (a product of Rogers & Kin), we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our automation tool to sync Etsy listings to Pinterest.

1. Data We Collect

Kintix only accesses the data necessary to perform the synchronization service:

  • Etsy Data: We access your shop listings, images, and product descriptions via the Etsy API to create Pins.
  • Pinterest Data: We access your board names and authorization tokens to publish Pins on your behalf.

2. How We Use Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your accounts via OAuth.
  • Automate the creation of Pinterest Pins based on your Etsy shop inventory.

3. Data Storage & Security

Kintix stores authorization tokens securely. We do not store your Etsy or Pinterest passwords. You can revoke access at any time through your respective Etsy or Pinterest account settings.
